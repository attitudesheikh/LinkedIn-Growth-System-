Layer 1
Market Intelligence
01 Trend Radar Every Monday
Spot emerging conversations before they peak. Feed output into Skills #9 or #12.
Claude Prompt
You are a LinkedIn market intelligence analyst. My niche: [YOUR NICHE / INDUSTRY] My ICP (Ideal Customer Profile): [WHO YOU SERVE] Task: Identify the top 10 emerging trends in my niche RIGHT NOW. For each trend provide: 1. Trend name & one-line description 2. Why it's gaining traction (3 bullet points) 3. Save trend outputs in a running doc. After 4 weeks you'll see patterns — recurring pain points = your best content pillars.
